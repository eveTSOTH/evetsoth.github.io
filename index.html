The Sound Of Thunder

"Who Dares Wins"

HQ: Amo, Metropolis

The Sound Of Thunder is a small gang PVP and industry alliance in EVE Online. We are currently based in the Amo system in Metropolis. Our core philosophy is independence and self reliance. We refuse to join or comply with the existing blue donut superpowers, and have committed ourselves to fighting against their dominance and ultimately a return to the old days when nullsec was lots of little independent alliances battling it out.
