Merc Services

 

The Sound Of Thunder is proud to present our new mercenary service. We are now accepting contracts, no job to small! Some of the services we offer

  • Standard wardec + attack your chosen target
  • Structure bashing/structure defence
  • Protection for mining fleets
  • Consultancy
  • Infiltration of a target corp
  • ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance, Recon)

 

Price

TSOTH believes in a fair pricing model, where you only pay for what we deliver. As such we generally operate a pay-per-kill pricing model.

 

Standard wardec

  • Wardec fee
  • Pay-Per-Kill
    • 1M ISK per Frigate kill
    • 2M ISK per Destroyer kill
    • 5M ISK per Cruiser kill
    • 30M ISK per Battlecruiser kill
    • 80M ISK per Battleship kill
    • 500M ISK per Carrier/Dread kill
    • 5B ISK per Supercarrier/Titan kill

 

Structue Bash/Structure Defence (Currently only Medium structures)

  • Wardec fee
  • 1B ISK deposit (this will be refunded to you if we fail to protect/kill the structure in question)
  • 500M on completion of our objective (either defending your structure or destroying your target strucutre)

 

The pricing for other services such as consultancy, corp infiltration, and intelligence gathering will vary depending on the specific requirements. We will work out a deal that suits both parties in advance.
