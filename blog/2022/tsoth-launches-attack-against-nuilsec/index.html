TSOTH Declares War Against The Blue Donut

October 10, 2022

2022   ·   pvp   warfare  

The Sound Of Thunder has started military operations against all the major nullsec powers in EVE, launching simultaneous wardecs against Pandemic Horde and Goonswarm Federation. For months there had been rumours of a planned winter offensive but CEO Carlos Warren had always refused to comment. With todays announcement, he has finally revealed more detail

“This campaign has been in the planning stages for a long time. Myself and the other TSOTH directors feel that now is the time for TSOTH to aggressively strike at the existing power structures of nullsec. Our corporation is known for its strong views on this issue. TSOTH’s vision for nullsec is that it should be lots of little independent groups all battling it out, rather than a stagnant world of superpowers and blue donuts. So today we are launching this attack against the two current main superpowers (Panfam and Goons) with the aim of inflicting severe damage to their power base. I will be honest, and admit I don’t think we can completely dismantle these powerblocs alone. Obviously we will try but it may not be achievable. However, we think that once people start to hear of our efforts, when word spreads that a small independent group has stood up to the blue donut and said enough is enough, it will inspire other groups to rise up and do the same.
